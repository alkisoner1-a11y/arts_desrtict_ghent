THE ARTS
DISTRICT GHENT

We are a creative collective driving the cultural heartbeat of Ghent. Our mission is to transform urban spaces into living canvases.

The 2026
Campaign

Our current campaign, "Please Touch The Art," challenges the traditional boundaries of galleries. We believe art should not just be observed from a distance, but deeply felt, experienced, and physically interacted with.

Through a series of immersive installations, live performances, and guerrilla exhibitions, we are bringing the boldest contemporary artists directly to the streets and warehouses of Ghent.

Our
Organization

Founded in 2020, Arts Destrict Ghent is a non-profit coalition of curators, artists, and city planners. We operate at the intersection of urban design and fine art.

With the support of local communities and international visionaries, we host over 50 events annually, providing a platform for both emerging local talent and established global icons.
